Overview

WELCOME TO PIRAMAL REVANTA - THE EDEN

Piramal Revanta is launching a new tower - The Eden, a residential address at the edge of the lush Sanjay Gandhi National Park in Mulund. The Eden features spacious 1, 2, 3, & 4 BHK homes & studio flats. These residences are elegantly styled, offer Vastu-compliant layouts, and bestow stunning views of rolling greens at SGNP. The property also houses lovely parks, over 30% open green spaces, and state-of-the-art amenities. 35+ amenities within the project include 2 clubhouses, a cafe, a gym, an indoor game room, a spa, a pool, and more to offer the best of fitness & leisure!
The surrounding locale further places you at the heart of Mulund, where every key landmark and transport routes ensure a convenient life. Starbucks, Fortis Hospital, St. Mary's High School, Nahur & Mulund Railway Stations, and Eastern Express Highway are all nearby. With spectacular residences in such a locale, Piramal Revanta The Eden brings you a refreshing lifestyle in the countryside of Mulund.
